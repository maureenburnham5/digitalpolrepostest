How to Bake Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients you will need:

  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 1/2 sticks of butter
  • 3/4 cup of packed light brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 12 ounce bag of semi-sweet chocolate chips

    • Directions:

    1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees, get out two cookie pans and cover with parchment paper.
    2. Whisk together the flour, baking soda, and 1 teaspoon of salt in a large bowl.
    3. Beat butter and both sugars in large bowl until light and fluffy. While beating gradually add your eggs and vanilla extract.
    4. Then add your flour mixture to the bowl and chocolate chips mix all ingredients until you get a dough.
    5. scoop 12 tablespoons of dough, roll individual scoops in hands then transfer on to pans, make sure they are 2 inches apart.
    6. put trays in ovene for 12 to 15 minutes.
    7. After baking place sheet out and let cool for a few minutes.
    8. Enjoy your cookies!
